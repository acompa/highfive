Terms

HighFive takes your privacy very seriously. Thus, you can count on the following:

1. Your Twitter credentials will only be used to log into Twitter. They are not stored locally.

2. The content in your Twitter timeline will not be saved–we only need the links in your timeline.

3. Your direct messages will not be accessed at all.

HighFive will, however, store the username of the person who tweeted the link. We will only use this information to improve our link recommendations. We will not use this information to engage in any uncool behavior, such as tweeting at your friends about HighFive or free iPads.
